Comments, Subscriptions, and Feeds, Oh my!

D. Ben Knoble on 23 Jul 2019 in Announcement

I’ve added comments, and subscriptions, and moved the feed to be more accessible.

Feed Me

The RSS feed has always been located right at /feed.xml. This hasn’t changed. What has is that a direct link is now provided in the footer.

Subscribe Me

Update 2020 April 14th: Feedburner got rid of email subscriptions

Using feedburner, I have put together and email subscription service. Simply click subscribe here or in the footer.

Comment on Me

I’ve opted to use Disqus to put comments on the blog. Enjoy. I’ll be putting a comment policy together at some point and posting it, but for now use the following guidelines:

  • be nice
  • stay on topic

You have been warned.
